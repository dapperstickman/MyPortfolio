Pop Up

A pop up that reminds you to get off your computer. We want to limit the amount of time we spend on the computer, so we set a timer than makes a pop up to remind us to get up for 30 minutes. There is a "I'm in the middle of a project button" that allows you to continue going if needed, but otherwise the popup won't go away unless you take a 30 minute break.
