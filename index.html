My Pop Up

Welcome to My Pop Up!



My Pop Up is a timed alarm for your browser that reminds you to take a break from the computer.

It's easy to lose track of time, so this pop up will set off a sound that will remind you how long you've been working so that you can take the strain off your eyes and go get something to energize yourself so you can get back to working efficiently. Simply click the button to set the timer and we will automatically remind you to take a break!
